Navigation

Navigation bar is used to navigate through the web apps or websites. This navigation component is responsive. It is a hamburger menu in medium and small devices.

    USES:
    1. Burger section- to show hamburger in M,S,XS devices, it will be hidden on L, XL devices

    2. Brand logo- It will be displayed in L,XL devices and hidden on M,S,XS devices

    3. Brand title- It will be displayed in M,S,XS devices and hidden on L,XL devices

    4. Search bar.
    etc..
